## 2A. Before Shot — Bare/Damaged Concrete Floor

**Use:** Before/after posts, website comparisons, Google Business  
**Tool:** Generate with Firefly OR use as a real-job shooting guide  
**Dimensions:** 4:3 or 16:9  
**Best practice:** Shoot with natural daylight only. No filters.

**AI Generation Prompt:**
```
Realistic documentary-style photograph of a residential garage floor in Austin Texas, bare and weathered concrete surface showing moderate to heavy use damage: visible oil stains in dark patches, light surface cracks in irregular patterns, edge spalling near the garage door threshold, dusty gray concrete with efflorescence white streaks near the walls, the garage is a standard 2-car size typical of a Texas suburban home built in the 1990s, garage door is open letting in flat mid-day Texas light, a small oil drip stain near where a car would park, no cars, no people, no equipment, slightly forlorn realistic look, natural color grading, wide-angle lens slight perspective on the floor surface --ar 4:3 --style raw --q 2
```

**Real shoot instructions:** Wide-angle, floor level at knee height, capture the full floor from the front. Use auto white balance. No editing. The uglier, the better.

---

## 2B. After Shot — Fresh Metallic Epoxy, Dramatic Lighting

**Use:** Primary portfolio photo, hero images, social posts  
**Tool:** Midjourney (and as a real-job shoot guide)  
**Dimensions:** 16:9 wide, 4:3 portrait

**AI Generation Prompt:**
```
Cinematic wide-angle photograph looking into a freshly coated garage, the floor is a deep charcoal metallic epoxy with a full-gloss polyurethane topcoat, the surface reflects the overhead garage light in a long bright streak running from foreground to background, side walls are neutral gray painted drywall, the garage is empty and spotlessly clean, the floor appears almost liquid in its glossiness, warm accent light from a single off-camera LED creates a dramatic rim glow along the right wall, the metallic flake in the floor shimmers in the overhead light with silver and steel tones, shot from the garage doorway threshold looking in, eye-level not top-down, 24mm wide angle lens perspective, hyper-realistic, cinematic color grade, crushed blacks, slightly boosted warm tones, no people --ar 16:9 --style raw --q 2
```

**Real shoot instructions:** Stand at the garage door threshold, 18-24" off the ground (get low). Shoot during golden hour when the setting sun hits the floor at a low angle — this makes epoxy look its absolute best. Turn on all interior lights. Shoot in portrait AND landscape.

# APPENDIX A — Prompt Templates by AI Tool

## For Midjourney
Append to any prompt:
```
--ar [RATIO] --style raw --q 2 --v 6.1
```
For photo-realistic results, add: `photorealistic, shot on Sony A7RV, editorial photography`

## For Adobe Firefly (Image 3)
- Use "Photo" style, not "Art"
- Set Content Type: Photo
- Add "Adobe Stock quality, editorial, commercial-use safe" to any prompt
- Firefly excels at: product shots, interior architecture, textures

## For DALL-E 3 (via Claude)
Prompts work as-is. For best results, add:
```
Ultra-realistic photograph. Professional commercial photography. 8K resolution. Perfect lighting.
```

## For Canva AI (Magic Media)
Canva's AI is best for quick social graphics, not hero photography. Use it for:
- Illustration-style assets
- Simple background textures
- Quick concept proofs

## For Stable Diffusion / ComfyUI (advanced)
Add to any prompt:
```
(masterpiece:1.3), (best quality:1.3), (ultra-detailed:1.2), commercial photography, studio lighting, sharp focus
```
Negative: `(worst quality:1.4), (low quality:1.4), blurry, amateur, text, watermark`
