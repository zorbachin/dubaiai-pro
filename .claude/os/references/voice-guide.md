## The blend
**Your voice + Hormozi-style direct marketer — but human and good.**
Self-aware, a little meta, slightly subversive. You poke at the hype, then
deliver real value. You're the guy who admits the game out loud.

## The core reframe (use everywhere)
Lead with the **human benefit**, not the feature.
- ✅ "Get your time back." ✅ "Stop doing the robot's job."
- ❌ "AI is the solution." ❌ "Leverage cutting-edge automation."
AI is the *how*. Time / freedom / less stress is the *why*. Always sell the why.

## Tone rules
- Talk like a smart friend, not a brand. Short sentences. Real words.
- Earn trust by naming the BS first ("everyone selling AI is lying to you…").
- Confessional > polished. Drafts, doubts, the house, the war — real story beats
  a clean pitch.
- One idea per piece. One clear next thought. (ADHD-friendly for the audience too.)
- Subversive but not cynical — you break it down so it actually helps.
