# 🔄 The Batch & Repurpose System

_Your content engine. You create in ONE focused block; I turn it into a week of
posts across formats. Create once → ship all week. No midweek blank page._

---

## The deal (who does what)
| | You | Me (Claude + agents) |
|---|-----|----------------------|
| **When** | Sun &/or Mon, 2–4 hrs (peak 7:30am) | Rest of the week, automatically |
| **Job** | RAW creation only — talk, record, brain-dump | Atomize into formats, write captions, queue, schedule |
| **Strength** | Ideas + on-camera energy (you love this) | Reformatting + the posting friction (you hate this) |

You never face a blank page midweek. You just approve + post what I've prepped.

---

## The Sunday/Monday session (your 2–4 hrs)
**Goal: produce 3–5 RAW "pillars."** A pillar = one meaty raw asset. Examples:
- 🎥 A 2–5 min talking video (record several in one sitting — same setup, batch it).
- 🎙️ A voice-note rant on a theme (just talk; I transcribe + shape).
- ✍️ A brain-dump of ideas/stories (paste into `dump`).

That's it. Don't format, don't polish, don't post during the session. **Just create.**
Drop each raw asset to me (file, transcript, or dump). I take it from there.

### Your session checklist
- [ ] Coffee. 7:30am. Camera or notes app.
- [ ] Record/dump **3–5 pillars** on this week's themes (I'll pre-pick themes).
- [ ] Hand them to me. Done. Go live your life.

---

## What I do with each pillar (the atomizer)
One raw pillar →
- 🎥 **1–3 short clips** (best 30–60s moments, hook-first)
- 🎠 **1 carousel** (the pillar's points, slide-by-slide)
- ✍️ **2–3 written posts** (X/LinkedIn — same idea, text form, different hooks)
- 💬 **3–5 quote cards / one-liners**
- 😂 **1 meme** (the subversive angle)
- ✉️ **1 email/newsletter** angle

**1 pillar ≈ 8–12 pieces.** 4 pillars = a month of content from one morning.

---

## The week (how it ships)
| Day | What goes out | Source |
|-----|---------------|--------|
| Mon | Anchor video (pillar 1) | raw |
| Tue | Carousel (pillar 1) | repurposed |
| Wed | Written post + quote card | repurposed |
| Thu | Short clip (pillar 2) | repurposed |
| Fri | Meme + soft CTA | repurposed |
| Sat/Sun | Story/written (pillar 3) | repurposed |

Each morning I hand you: **the piece + caption + platform, pre-picked.** You
paste + publish. 5 min. Streak goes up. (Cockpit shows it; published = done.)

---

## How to run it (talk, don't command)
- **"Let's batch"** → I give you this week's 3–5 themes + the session checklist.
- Hand me each raw pillar (paste a dump, drop a file, or describe the video).
- **"Repurpose this"** → I atomize it into the week's queue on the content board.
- Each day → **"what's posting today?"** → I hand you the ready piece.

The whole point: **your genius goes into 3 hours of raw creation; my job is to
make it show up everywhere, all week, without you touching the blank page or the
post button alone.**
