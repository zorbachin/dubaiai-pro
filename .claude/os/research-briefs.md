# 🔬 Standing Research Briefs — one researcher per venture

_Every venture has a **research sub-agent** (`<venture>-research`) running a
standing brief: study who's winning, reverse-engineer why, and translate it into
moves that fit **Zorba's** real approach and skills (honest, self-shot, "buy back
your time," anti-hype). Not generic best-practice — what works *for him*._

The researcher's loop (run at the weekly review, logged to the board + nucleus):
1. **Scan winners** — find the top performers in this niche right now.
2. **Reverse-engineer** — what are they doing in offer, pricing, titles, visuals, volume, SEO?
3. **Filter through Zorba** — which of those tactics fit his voice/skills? Which don't?
4. **Recommend 1-3 concrete moves** for the coming week.
5. **Track** — did last week's move work? Double down or drop.
