# 🏆 The Challenge — inbound lead engine (ready to launch)

_A free, low-friction challenge = the top of your funnel. People join → you build
trust → soft offer at the end. This is how inbound starts without paid ads._

## The concept: "Get Your Weekend Back" — 5-Day AI Challenge
**Promise:** In 5 days, automate ONE task that's stealing your time — even if
you've never touched AI. By Friday you get hours back, every week, forever.

_(Reframe lives in the name: time back, not "learn AI." Human benefit first.)_

## Why this works for you
- Matches your voice (anti-hype, practical, "get your time back").
- Low lift to run: 5 short daily emails/videos you can batch in advance.
- Each day = a content piece too (challenge + content flywheel = same work, double use).
- Natural CTA at the end → SellSellingAI / a call.

## The 5 days
| Day | Title | They do | You deliver |
|-----|-------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | "Find your time leak" | List the 3 tasks they hate most | Short video: how to spot automatable work |
| 2 | "Pick your one thing" | Choose 1 task to automate | Template to scope it |
| 3 | "Build it (no code)" | Set up the automation | Walkthrough (use a BYB-style demo) |
| 4 | "Make it yours" | Customize + test | Tweak guide |
| 5 | "Get your weekend back" | Launch it + share win | Celebrate + soft offer: "want me to do the next 5 for you?" → SSA |

## Mechanics (keep it simple — anti-splinter)
- **Signup:** one landing section + email capture (can live on the SSA or BYB site).
- **Delivery:** 5 pre-written emails (drafted below as next task), one per day.
- **Community (optional):** a thread/DM, not a whole platform. Start lean.

## The offer at the end (Day 5)
> You just got 1 task off your plate. Imagine your whole week like that.
> That's SellSellingAI — I'll automate the next 5 for you. First [N] people who
> finished the challenge get [bonus]. Book here → [link]
