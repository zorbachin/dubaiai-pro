_The closing tool (snap a photo, get a finished-room render + full quote in ~90
seconds) is a perfect cold-outreach product for trades. Lead with their pain
(being first with the quote wins). Personalize the first line per business.
NEVER blast generic. No em-dashes._

## The play
- Target one trade at a time (painters first). The render demo is most dramatic for visual trades (paint, epoxy, landscaping, remodels).
- Personalize line 1 to each business (their actual work / city). One specific line = the difference between personal and spam.
- Soft ask: the 90-second video or a 15-min look. The video IS the pitch.
- Always include the opt-out line. Keeps it clean and legal.
