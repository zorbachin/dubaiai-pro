# MIKLAT GAMES — Support & Monetization Plan v1
Frame: solidarity product. People don't pay to play — they pay so the NEXT
family plays free. Never break: free gameplay, no ads, works offline.

## The funnel (emotion-first, never interruptive)
1. EARN THE ASK: support prompts appear only at peaks — after a personal
   best / daily-challenge win / 3rd session. One line on end screens:
   "Free because people like you keep it that way. 🕯️ Keep the lights on"
2. THE TZEDAKAH BOX (support page, /support/ on the portal):
   - 🥙 FALAFEL — one-time $5: name on the "Wall of the Miklat"
   - 🛡️ CHAI — one-time $18 (chai = life, the culturally obvious amount):
     golden shield by your name + 1,000 miklat.wallet shekels
   - 🏠 MAMAD — $5/month: golden savta cosmetic, doors open 48h early,
     name in credits
3. WALLET BRIDGE: supporters receive miklat.wallet shekels — the economy
   (paid CONTINUE, Iron Dome v19+) becomes the first real-money sink.
4. SPONSOR A SHELTER (the unity twist): donations display as city pride —
   "A supporter in Tel Aviv keeps Sderot playing." Ties into the 10-city map.
## Hard rules
- No paywalled gameplay. No ads. Nothing between a kid in a shelter and play.
- Copy never guilt-trips; it invites. "Forever" promises removed site-wide.
