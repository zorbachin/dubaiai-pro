# REPS IN PEACE — Visual Style Profile

## ART STYLE
Stylized 3D animation, Pixar-Tim Burton hybrid. Slight cel-shading on skeletons. Semi-realistic gym environments with painterly textures and dust haze.

## COLOR PALETTE (locked)
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Bone white | #F5F1E8 | Skeletons, text, highlights |
| Crimson red | #B0152F | Coach's tank, accent, blood/drip motif |
| Charcoal black | #1A1717 | Backgrounds, shadows |
| Dusty gold | #C49A5A | Rim lighting, light shafts |
| Concrete gray | #6C6963 | Platform, walls |

## LIGHTING
- Hard side lighting with **visible dust motes** in every shot
- Warm overhead key light (3200K equivalent)
- Cool blue ambient fill (5600K equivalent) — creates the cinematic temperature contrast
- Crimson rim light specifically reserved for **Coach Skully menace moments**

## CAMERA
- Coach Skully: low angle, hero framing
- Bonesy: eye-level, slightly higher to make him look smaller
- Two-shots: wide, rule of thirds, Coach left, Bonesy right (consistent)
- Pattern interrupts (re-hooks): direct-to-camera medium close-ups, single eye-socket lit

## CHARACTER POSITIONING RULES
- **Coach Skully is ALWAYS on the left** of any two-shot
- **Bonesy is ALWAYS on the right**, slightly behind, slightly smaller
- They never cross frame mid-scene

## ENVIRONMENT — "The Crypt Gym"
The channel's only set. Memorize it:
- Single iron squat rack, rusted, center-left of the room
- Pull-up bar mounted on exposed brick wall, back-right
- Chalk-dusted lifting platform under the rack
- One hanging incandescent bulb (warm key)
- Dumbbell rack in background (out of focus)
- Concrete floor, exposed brick walls
- A single barbell always present somewhere in frame
- Kettlebell rack (3 sizes) added in Leija-blend update

## CONSISTENCY RULES (every shot must contain)
1. Visible dust in light shafts
2. Either the iron rack or pull-up bar in frame (or both)
3. Both characters in frame **OR** a clean cut between solo shots
4. The crimson-bone-charcoal palette dominates — no off-palette colors
5. Slight film grain overlay on every clip

## RE-HOOK VISUAL SIGNATURE
Every re-hook (4 per long-form episode) uses this exact composition:
- Medium close-up on Coach Skully
- Single crimson rim light from camera left
- Dark gym fully in shadow behind
- Coach leans slightly into camera
- Hold 3 seconds, then hard cut to next scene

This visual repetition trains the viewer that "when Coach leans in, the truth is coming."
