## CALL-TO-ACTION HIERARCHY

Priority order for every video's end card:

2. **Watch the next episode** — drives session time and binge-watch behavior
3. **Comment your worst squat mistake** — drives engagement signal
4. **Save the video** — drives the YouTube save-rate signal (high weight)
