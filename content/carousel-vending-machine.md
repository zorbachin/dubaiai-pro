# Carousel — "Stop using AI like a vending machine"

**Use:** Instagram + LinkedIn carousel (also works as a Substack Note thread).
**Why this one:** teaching/value carousels earn *saves & shares* (the engagement that compounds), and it carries your brand — practical, time-back, "chief of staff not chatbot."
**Export:** open `carousel-vending-machine.html`, screenshot each slide (or drop the text into Canva). 8 slides.

---

### Slide 1 — HOOK
**Stop using AI like a vending machine.**
Use it like a chief of staff.

### Slide 2
A vending machine: you ask, it drops one thing, you walk away.
That's about 5% of what it can do.

### Slide 3
A chief of staff knows your week, your goals, your inbox —
and quietly runs the boring parts so you don't have to.

### Slide 4 — EXAMPLE 1: Email
Don't say "write an email."
Paste the whole messy thread and say:
*"Tell me the decision needed, who owns the next step, and a reply I can send right now."*
→ 15 minutes becomes 2.

### Slide 5 — EXAMPLE 2: Decisions
Don't ask "should I do X?"
Say: *"Here's my situation. Argue both sides honestly, then tell me what you'd do and why."*
→ A thinking partner, not a yes-man.

### Slide 6 — EXAMPLE 3: Your day
*"Here's everything on my plate. What are the 3 things that actually move the needle today — and what can wait until tomorrow?"*
→ Start the day pointed in the right direction.

### Slide 7 — THE SHIFT
Stop *fetching answers.*
Start *handing off work.*
That's where the hours come back.

### Slide 8 — CTA
I build this into businesses — so the stuff you hate doing just… gets done.
If there's a task you dread, that's where we start.
