# 📡 The Build Log: the brief productivity newsletter

_Companion to Zorbasphere (Substack). Where Zorbasphere is the cinematic deep
story, The Build Log is the opposite: super brief, fast, useful. What I built,
one idea, the next goal, and a time-saver you can steal. Under 60 seconds to read._

**Cadence:** weekly. **Length:** ~150 words, scannable. **Promise:** a quick
build update plus one thing you can use today.

---

## The template (reusable)

> **THE BUILD LOG · No. 00X**
>
> 🔨 **Building**: [1 to 2 lines: what I shipped or am building right now]
>
> 💡 **Idea**: [one insight, a sentence or two]
>
> 🎯 **Next**: [the goal I am aiming at]
>
> ⏱️ **Steal this**: [one AI tip or tool. If it is a prompt, give the prompt. If it is a tool, name it and one line on why it saves time.]
>
> That is it. More next week.
> Zorba · letaidoit.pro
