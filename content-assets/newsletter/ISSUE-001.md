## ⏱️ Three Two-Minute Time-Savers

_Tap a prompt to run it. Steal them all._

### 1. 💼 WORK — Kill the email thread that won't die
That 14-reply thread where nobody's said the actual decision? Make AI do it.

> **Prompt:** *"Read this email thread and tell me, in 3 lines: the decision that needs making, who owns the next step, and a short reply I can send right now. [paste the thread]"*

Two minutes instead of fifteen. You look on top of it; you spent no time being on top of it.

### 2. 🌅 LIFE — Plan the perfect night out (no doom-scrolling)
Stop opening five tabs to plan one evening.

> **Prompt:** *"I've got [tonight / a budget of $X] in [your city] and I'm in the mood for [chill / fun / a date]. Don't give me options — give me ONE specific plan: where to go, when, and what to order."*

One plan. No decision fatigue. Out the door.

### 3. 💪 HEALTH — Turn your fridge into dinner (or a workout into 12 minutes)
Two of my favorites:

> **Prompt (fridge → meal):** *"Here's what's in my fridge: [list]. Give me one high-protein meal I can cook in 15 minutes with no shopping."*

> **Prompt (no-gym workout):** *"I've got 12 minutes and a pull-up bar. Give me ONE circuit — no fluff, no equipment shopping — that leaves me sweaty."*

(That second one's how I train at the park. Works.)
